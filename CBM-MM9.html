CLASS BASED MODIFICATION GUIDE

The Mega Man 9 category covers the characters from the original Mega Man 9/Rockman 9 game.


CONCRETE
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Peel protector class, focused on debuffing enemies. 155 0.6/0.58 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Fire a spread of concrete that creates solid blocks on your path. If you hit an enemy with your shots you will disable their jumps temporarily. A dashing tackle. Pressing it close to one of your concrete blocks will lift it allowing you to throw it as a projectile for extra damage, and it can be charged to increase radius. Can be charged to instead become a stomp that stun close enemies. None None
Info Tips
Even though Concrete's damage isn't that high, you hold a lot of tools for making your enemies be ineffective. Concrete shot can prevent them from jumping, the bash can send them flying backwards, the drop can stun them and throw can be effectively used for crowd control. Having team members around makes you pretty valuable. Concrete can use the concrete blocks in multiple ways. Preventing someone from jumping and blocking their path with more blocks, and you can still use blocks while holding a concrete block on your hand, so there is no reason not to keep firing. Also, your bash dash can pass over blocks without the need to navigate it.

TORNADO
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
AOE Trapping indirect DPS class, focused on aerial control. 85 0.76/0.74 12
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Shoots smalls tornados that stick to surfaces. Cause slow on those that touch them. Can only keep 4 of them. Creates a huge turbilion of wind that sends close by foes flying up. Will send the set tornadoes flying again. Toggles Tornado Mode On/Off. Holding jump while on Tornado Mode will make you float. Jumping on enemy's heads while on Tornado Mode will stomp them causing a lot of damage. None
Info Tips
While on open areas, Tornado will be focusing mostly on harassing people with the tornado stomp and pushing enemies away with the close range tornado blow and horizontal tornado mines. But when you can grant that your targets will be hit by your ceiling tornado mines, you will be setting them up and using the close range tornado blow to launch them up for dunking. If you find it too hard to stomp on people with your tornado stomp, you can always setup ground tornado mines and launch them up under you for some easy damage while you fly through the level.

WOMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Buff support class, focused on team stats buff. 90 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Fires a ripping projectile. Sing to use buffs:

Red: Attack Buff;

Green: Healing;

Yellow: Speed Buff.

If you hold it will instead become an AOE effect. Just tap to use the self version.		 None Press jump while airborn to swin underwater.
Info Tips
The water bubble is a key tool for use for terrain dominance. Summoning it on a closed space can throw everyone there off, since the altered physics (And how certain classes behave in water) can take people by surprise if they are too concentrated on the fight. This confusion is your chance to strike by surprise. If you remember that water physics also affect jump speed and height you can connect that being able to summon water anywhere can open access to new places and positions. Capitalized on that by giving your team some boosts.

PLUGMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Tether support class, focused on strafing combat. 100 0.75/0.73 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Shoots electricity balls that crawl on the ground and ricochet on walls. Shoots a plug that connects to any target. Press Main Fire to activate your special effect.

On enemies it will cause constant damage and will recharge your ammo.

On allies will use your ammo to increase their attack speed.		 None None
Info Tips
Plug has to commit to 3 main styles of play and really go for it. The first one is harassing people with Plug Balls and hidding to recharge. This is mostly for times where you have too many enemies around. When dueling, the second style is switching in between hitting with your Plug Balls and using your Plug Tether to steal energy from your enemy. If you have your team around though, you will use the third style, where you will use your Plug Tether to buff your teammate's attacks. Plug Balls actually fly in an arc and are affected by gravity. This means that you will probably not be snipping people, but is very good to deal with uneven terrain and obstacles. The ricochet also lasts long enough for you to overwhelm people.

JEWELMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Combination indirect DPS class, focused on custom attacks. 100 0.76/0.74 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Activates Jewel Satelite. It denies missiles. Pressing again sends it forward. Changes the Jewels that form your Jewel Satelite.

Cyan: Damage Increase.

Green: Ricochet.

Red: Explosion.

Blue: Trail.

Yellow: Speed.

Purple: Make it fall thanks to gravity.		 Resets all your gems to the Cyan set. None
Info Tips
Jewel's custom main fire will be your main preocupation when using this class. Don't foolishly use a single combo as it will become predictable and easy to counter. Also, keep in mind that every single jewel type as have their own damage multiplier, so more useful gems will most likely have lower damage. Learn the possible effects certain combos can have.

More Greens: Add mode ricochets;

More Reds: Increase the explosion's range;

More Blues: Increase the duration/density of the trail;

More Yellows: Increase Speed;

More Purples: Increase gavity's effect;

Red + Blue: Causes the trail to explode;

Blue + Purple: Cause your trail to fall on the ground.

HORNET
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Homing indirect DPS class, focused on chasing. 100 0.8/0.78 13
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Sends flying hornets that follow your eyesight. Press Alt-fire to send them homing on the nearest target. Hold Mainfire to keep them in place, allowing you to change their tragectory and delay their flight. Throws hive on the ground. It will spread honey on the ground that causes slow. None Can hover by holding Jump.
Info Tips
Hornet's game will be spent on the hunt. Moving around the level and choosing a target that you can isolate is the key to start a good fight, and following that, smart use of your passive hover and honey globs will allow you prevent any target from escaping your attacks. Remember that Hornet Chaser moves very fast when first launched, so wait for your hornets to get close enough to a target before starting using their homing ability. It can also help deal with cases where the enemy is hiding behind wall or obstacles to defend themselves from you homing attacks.

MAGMAMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Charger off-tank class, focused on keep away. 125 0.7/0.68 14
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Shoots a double spread from your left side. Gets weaker and smaller when out of ammo. Shoots a double spread from your right side. Gets weaker and smaller when out of ammo. Hold to recharge your ammo. Loses ammo when hurt.
Info Tips
Magma has good enough stats to actually win any trade you start with an advantage, but don't overextend it. Since charging your attacks requires you to be stationary, it is best to do trades in short burts. If you can't get the kill, just move away and try again later. The loss of firepower is too much to trade safely after your ammo is empty. One of Magma's main advantage is that they can do a lot of damage simply by shooting at crowds without too much care. If you are not being targeted in the moment, use any opportunity to unload your guns on the crowd go out for a charge.

FAKEMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Pressure direct DPS class, focused on trading. 100 0.9/0.88 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Fast shot that have a random spread. First shot of a series fly straight in a rapid fire voley. Shooting while using the Quick Jump will make all of them fly straight. If you are out of ammo, it will instead become a melee batton attack that stuns enemies. Reloads your magnum. A quick backward jump. Can confiscate items on the floor.
Info Tips
Fake's engagement is pretty strong. Don't get too afraid of using up all of your ammo. If you run out of ammo but aren't in a disadvantage yet, just hit them with your batton and strafe around while reloading to get a safe reload without loosing proximity. If you really need to disengage, just use the quick jump. If you really need a boost to your disengage, just jump back before using the quick jump, this way you will fly way further and will have enough time to reload before landing.
