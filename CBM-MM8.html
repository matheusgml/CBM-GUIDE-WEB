CLASS BASED MODIFICATION GUIDE

The Mega Man 8 category covers the characters from the original Mega Man 8/Rockman 8 game and it's remakes/spin-offs.


TENGUMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Anti-Air indirect DPS class, focused on air control. 100 0.76/0.74 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Tapping will just release a projectile that bounces on surfaces. Holding it will make you dash forward. The melee impact will dunk foes. Sends a tornado mine that slowly crawls on the ground. Will trap foest or can be used as a lift for allies. None Press Jump again in midair to hover.
Info Tips
With Tengu, you will be looking for ways to dunk foes. Either use your Tornado Hold to lift them, or camp flying enemies, but your main damage output will be in throwing foes down to the ground. A good combo is to use your hove to approach an enemy from above and than try to get them into a Tornado Hold, after that, use your dash to hit them back to the ground. Low ceilings aren't actually a bad thing for this class. Locking your foes on the ceiling will make your job trying to dunk on them even easier. So don't be discouraged from going after them on closed spaces.

ASTROMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Turret indirect DPS class, focused on crowd management. 85 0.75/0.73 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Shoots a small projectile. First release on a series will create a clone that attacks on the direction you are firing at. Releases a barrage of meteors on the arena. Toggles Hovering On/Off. Can float above the ground.
Info Tips
Astro's floaty mobility and turret skills are important to keep your foes occupied while you charge your Astro Crush. Fighting against crowds is the best choice for Astro, since you will be having the most chances of hitting a lot of people with a single combo. If you need to put multiple Copy Visions on the field, you can easily do that by laying one and stop attacking, making it charge again in 5 seconds less than if your kept shooting. Just remember that after you create a new Copy Vision, the earlier one will stop following your attack directions.

SWORDMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Duelist off-tank class, focused on remote dueling. 125 0.75/0.72 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A melee attack. It causes extra damage if you use it on the air. Detaches your upper body sending it flying forward. If follows your aim. You can switch your POV to your upper body by pressing Main Fire. Pressing Alt-fire cancels it. It loses speed if it goes for too long. None None
Info Tips
Sword's alt-fire is the main reason for this class to so dangerous. The best way to start a trade is to first use your alt-fire to single a target out and take out as much as you can before having to start using your melee slashes. Once you lock them on a favorable place, go all out with your attacks. Both kinds of POV during your alt-fire their own utilities. You can either got for the Upper Half POV for more precise targeting and longe range sniping, or you can use the Lower Half POV for better strafing control and local awareness. Also, remember that you also cause damage while comming back to you lower body, so you can suprise certain enemies by hitting them on the way back.

CLOWNMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Tricky direct DPS class, focused on tricky movement. 85 0.93/0.91 12
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Normal weapon is a long range punch. Can swing on pegs. Second weapon will create pegs for you to swing around. While attacking you will spawn an electric aura that causes contact damage. Aura is stronger if you swinging. Will dig your hand into the ground and spawn it where you are aiming at, causing constant damage. Disable the electric aura while you are using it. None None
Info Tips
Clown's swing ability is the most important part of their kit. It is capable of big damage, has high mobility and allows you for more favorable positioning. Save your Peg Summoning for cases where you can either grant a hit from ramming into someone or an easy escape, otherwise trade using your basic Thunder Claw and hand trap. The minimun height for a peg summon is a little above 1 block height. If you want to ram into people using your electric field, learn to use that for your adanvatage as you will be staying close to the ground during most of the swing allowing you get a lot of damage.

SEARCH
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Camping indirect DPS class, focused on snipping. 80 0.7/0.68 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Will summon crossairs that will lock on people who cross it. Releasing it will send all of your homing missiles at the same time. Holding for too long will cause you to release the missiles. The missiles will cause more damage the longer they fly. Sends a ricocheting disk. Summons a wall of bushes for you to hide. While on the bushes, you are invisible. None
Info Tips
Search's main gimmick is hidding on your bushes to snipe people. Don't stay put on a single place. Walk around getting people on your aim and use the bushes to throw people off. If you are on a close encounter, use your alt-fire as a reliable attack, as you will be having a hard time getting your missiles locked at this close distance. You can actually keep 2 bush groups at the same time, so capitalize on that extra space to cause a lot confusion by jumping around six possible hidding spots. Use them as decoys too, to allow you some room to trick your opponents.

FROSTMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Crowd Control tank class, focused on snaring enemies. 200 0.5/0.48 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Sends an ice wave that can freeze foes on place and can crawl on surfaces. Can be delayed. Pressing Alt-fire will instead send a barrage of ice crystals that bounce on surfaces. Melee punch. None Suffers half hitstun.
Info Tips
Frost is very good in team games thanks to the highly useful Frost Wave, that is capable of freezing multiple enemies in place with a single attack. After you freeze an enemy, you can easily pummel them with your punches for heavy damage. The only downside is that you will be mostly effective against grounded enemies, so save your Frost Crystals to when you have to fight airborn enemies.

GRENADE
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Aggressive direct DPS class, focused on constant harassment. 100 0.74/0.72 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Shoots a grenade that causes a long lasting explosion. Lobs grenades that bounce on surfaces and explode on contact with enemies or after a set period of time. Nope Gains Rage by causing/taking damage. Rage improves your attacks and movement speed. Will cause a huge explosion if you die with Rage on.
Info Tips
Grenade is a very aggressive class, so you have to keep on the front lines, trying to get as much hits as possible, and you will readily rewarded for that. If you ever run out of Rage, try to reposition yourself so you get atleast 2 to 3 hits of advantage against your next opponent, allowing you to more easily access Rage again. There is really no downside to having a full Rage bar, so even if you are on low life in a team game, you can keep trading and with your improved damage output and try to explode on the middle of a crowd as a last ditch effort to get something out of it.

AQUAMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Area Control indirect DPS class, focused on pressure management. 100 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Releases glob of water. The globs fly further if you have more pressure. With full extra pressure, you will instead release a homing water snake. Builds up pressure. Extra pressure can't be held to. Using Alt-fire with full extra pressure will release a tower of water that causes a lot of damage and will break into a lot of small globs of water. None Canceling extra pressure build up while not at full charge is a taunt.
Info Tips
Aqua's gameplay revolves all around keeping your water pressure high. Not only is your normal attack improved by it, you will get a lot milleage of for the Full Pressure attacks. Just remember that those kinds of attacks are better saved for cases where you are either starting or finishing a kill, since they take a lot to use. Remember that you can start charging Full Pressure again until your current full pressure attack is done, so keep that in mind when you fight so that you can get the most use of your pressure in between attacks.

ORIGINAL
DUO
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Infantry off-tank class, focused on pushing enemy lines. 135 0.76/0.74 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Fires rapid fire short range punches. Giga Tackle: Short range punch. Can be charged to do a Giga Tackle.

Meteor Dive: Turns into a ricocheting meteor. Pressing Main Fire will make you redirect your trajectory and explode. Pressing Alt-fire will make you slam on the ground.		 None Suffer half hitstun.
Info Tips
Original Duo has a lot ways of invading enemy lines and displacing single targets out of fight for disposing of them. An easy strategy is to dive the enemy team with your Meteor Dive, than use your charged Giga Tackle to push the main DPS out of the crowd and take them out with your rapid fire punches. Your Meteor Dive has 3 main utilities that help you different situations. The direct hit from it will cause high damage and send your target flying backwards. The Main Fire explosion is a little more damaging and doesn't push your enemy back, allowing you for an easier follow up. The Alt-Fire slam causes a small AOE shockwave that allows you to hit multiple enemies.

EVIL
ROBOT
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Harassment copy class, focused on heavy artillery. 115 0.73/0.71 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A large shot that can be charged. Refills half of the ammo for your weapon and gives you Rage. Rage triples your Main Fire and allows you to use super attacks using your Alt-fire. None Cannot pick up ammo. Upon being killed drops one of it's parts randomly. If a player can pick up all 6 of it's parts in a single match, they will get a triple shot boost for as long as they keep themselves alive.
Info Tips
Evil Robot's style of combat is heavily focused on filling your Rage meter. Your main fire is easy enough to use that you will be able to competently trade before getting weapons, but you really should focus on getting the spread shots from the Rage alongside the Super Attacks most of the time. Your weaponless Rage Super Attack is actually a beam of energy that heavily debuffs your enemy. It is extremely useful and allow for some easy upsets if you smart with it.
