CLASS BASED MODIFICATION GUIDE - MM1

The Mega Man 1 category covers the characters from the original Mega Man/Rockman game and it's remakes/spin-offs.


CUTMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Mixups direct DPS class, focus on controling distance. 85 1.0/0.98 12
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A ricocheting boomerang projectile. Quick cutting slice. None Can Wall Jump up to 9 times before having to land.
Info Tips
Cut is a class all about the best use of your distance and positioning, using the wall jump and the ricocheting of the main projectile alongside good positioning can score you some easy kills. If you get into a closed space, don't fear that the lack of mobility will make you helpless. Use the ricocheting properties of your Main Fire to discourage the enemy from comming close until you are on more friendly ground.

GUTSMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Heavy off-tank class, focus on overwhelming your opponent. 185 0.56/0.54 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Summons a rock.

Press again to toss the rock, that will split into bits.		 A melee punch with heavy damage.

If you have a rock in hand, you will lay it on the floor.

Punching a rock will explode it into bits that fly on the direction you are looking.		 None Suffers less hitstun if you don't have a rock in hand.
Info Tips
Guts is a class that benefits from pressuring oponnents. Since too closed spaces limit their range, you will have to force favorable grounds by smart allocation of your set rocks. The rocks that are set on the ground have multiple purposes. Since they are solid objects, they can serve as either plataforms or walls to block incomming enemies. Keeping that in mind, if you are allowed to build up a spot for you, you will be able to safely harass your enemies without fear of being taken umprepared.

ICEMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Setup indirect DPS class, focus on territory control. 100 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Fast moving ice arrows that cause slowness.

Can be used to explode statues for medium damage.		 An ice beam that can either create statues, cause minor damage if targeted directly but causing slowness, or be aimed at the ceiling for an AOE attack that causes minor constant damage. None None
Info Tips
Ice is a class with a lot of use for preparing the field. Having your statues ready and well positioned will allow you to get some easy kills without having to aim too much. The statues are solid, so they also serve as walls or plataforms. A good strategy is to put some on access points and explode them when someone passes through. Since the explosion also applies the slow effect, you will be able to more easily trade with them. Statues that are exploded can also explode other statues, so you can easily setup chain reactions.

BOMBMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Mixup indirect DPS class, focus on keeping your target guessing. 125 0.65/0.63 13
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Throws a contact bomb forward. If you delay the release of the bomb to atleast halfway of it's fuse it will create a bigger explosion. Lobs a timed bomb forward. None The center of the explosion causes more damage.
Info Tips
Bomb is a class that is very hard to read, making it a great class to keep your targets guessing while you mixup your attacks, making them struggle to dodge. Bomb has a huge jump arc even though they are not a very fast class. This makes it easier to bait attacks and counter by setting a mix of contact and timed bombs to get some pretty easy surprise damage.

FIREMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Brawling direct DPS class, focus on constant DPS. 85 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A fireball that leaves a burning spot on the ground under where it hit.

While you fire it, you get surrounded by an AOE damage aura.		 A slow moving fire pilar wave with high damage that crawls on the ground. None None
Info Tips
Fire is a class that doesn't really depends on precision, being more of a "damage everything that comes close" kind of deal that makes it dangerous in closed spaces. Storm a group of enemies and do high damage to capitalize on that. The fire pilar wave is slow moving and can crawl on surfaces, meaning that it can deny tight passages with ease, and if your enemies are stuck in there, you can be assured they will take a lot of damage. Also, your Fire Storm will leave the burning spot exactly under where it hit, so if you hit a ceiling or wall, you will leave that spot on the floor right under it. Keep that in mind to spread some fire spots on hard to reach places.

ELECMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Assassin direct DPS class, focused on high poke damage. 75 0.9/0.88 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A splitting ripping projectile.

The angle you shoot changes the spread of the split.		 Instant dash that can cross some hard terrain.

You will cause damage if you land exactly on a target.		 None None
Info Tips
Elec is a class that works pretty well if you are at uneven altitudes, as it will make your shots fly closer (Flying almost straight together on vertical angles) and this means you can cause a ton of damage by abusing support mobility. Your dash can cross complicated terrains (low walls, stairs, elevations, etc), meaning that you don't really have to navigate all of that to chase after someone. So learning the distance your dash covers is the best means of cutting someone's escape short.

TIMEMAN

Peel indirect DPS class, focused on downgrading your target. 100 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Splitting arrows: 2 arrows that spread on a tight arc.

Sequential arrows: Sequential shots that fly at different speeds.		 An AOE slow debuff plus a speed buff for you.

If you press it exactly as you are going to get hit you will deny the damage. You will also get a small speed boost during the start of the time distortion.		 None None
Info Tips
Time is a class that excels with proper timing (Ha Ha). Using your Alt-fire is your main engage tool. Saving it to the first hit in a trade is key to getting you a safe kill. Your sequential arrows are actually pretty useful if you know how to use their alternating speeds to surprise your targets. Since your arrows cause high damage, you can force your enemy on an easier spot with the slower arrow and snipe them with the faster shot.

OILMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Terrain control direct DPS class, focused speed. 90 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A chargeable shot that will send a bouncing glob of oil on the ground that leaves a trail. The more you charge, the more it will bounce. You can step on the globs to surf on it. A very rapid fire attack that can be used to extend your surf ammo by laying globs on the ground right in front of you. Oil Ignite. It will ignite any of your remaining globs on the field. The globs you leave on the ground cause damage. They can be lit on fire to cause extra damage.
Info Tips
Oil is a class that can really become dangerous if you learn the surf ability. The speed and mobility gained from it will make you extremely hard to loose or to catch. If you prefer not to get close, hold Main Fire when stepping on the rideable blobs of oil to shoot them through the floor. Having a fire type class on your side can be useful to change your strategy. Covering the arena on oil blobs just to have some fire type class ignite them will create a minefield that will make your enemies suffer hell to navigate. You can still do it with Oil alone, but fire classes can speed up the process without having to sacrifice all of your globs.
