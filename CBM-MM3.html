MAGNET
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Anti mobility indirect DPS class, focused on guarding against diving. 100 0.8/0.78 12
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A homming projectile. Hold fire to force it to fly straight and build up power, release to make it home on the nearest enemy. Hold to push enemies back. The magnetism will build up with time, shortening the total time you have to hold to fire this weapon. None You will passively build up magnetism with time.
Info Tips
The magnet missiles are extremely good if you keep in mind that you can extend their strenght by holding Main Fire. To capitalize on the maximum distance when being approached by enemies, prepare to use your Alt-fire, sending them away and go back to use the maximum distance for strong shots. Remember that you can passively get magnetic energy for you Alt-Fire, meaning you will have to charge for less time before being able to use it. Something that is interesting, even if you can't use your Alt-fire to finish kills, you can throw enemies off easily, meaning that they will have atleast 1 less kill on their numbers.

GEMINI
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Turret indirect DPS class, focused on positioning. 100 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A medium speed shot. Fires a wall bouncing laser.

While your clone is active, forces it to stay on a single place or summons it back to you.		 Summons a clone that follows you firing automatically at any enemy close. None
Info Tips
Your clone attacks by itself when anyone comes close to it, so a good strategy is to bring it along when you are being chased, and plant it somewhere when you are safer. Smart application of the clone can be both used offensively and defensively. Be warned though, your team's shots can hurt it too (including your's). It may sound obvious, but a good plan is always using the clone as a decoy. Also, if you prematurely disable it using the item, it only costs half of the bar instead of the full bar, so if your clone is still alive and you need the Gemini Laser, just disable it with the item and save the ammo.

HARDMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Diving tank class, focused on frontline. 200 0.5/0.48 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Shoots knuckles that return on contact. They can only cause damage once per shot. Boosts upward to make the Quake Press, the higher you get, the more damage it causes. Can be held to increase height. None Suffers half hitstun
Info Tips
Hard is practically the tankiest you can get under standard circumstances. Since this class has an amazing fight starter (with the Quake Press), you can surprise your enemies with an out of nowhere AOE attack and throw them off for your team to capitalize. Since your Quake Press gets stronger the higher you get, you can get some boost to your jump (from either items, higher plataforms or buffs from your allies) and grant a maximum damage drop every time. And don't forget, you can start it while jumping.

TOPMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Dashing direct DPS class, focused on timing. 85 1.1/1.08 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A spinning dash. Charging it increases distance/damage/ duration. The dash has a 75% chance of fully dodging homing attacks. Sends 3 spreading projectiles. None None
Info Tips
Since your movement is highly connected to your main method of attack, you will be hugging your enemies while charging your dash for some fast damage and release it for either a scape or to finalize a kill. Taking in consideration you can dodge homing weapons, you are pretty hard to throw off. Low charge dashes are actually a pretty good tool for chasing. Since you lose a little mobility while charging and the strongest dash is hard to control, throw the lower charges constantly to chase down enemies.

SNAKEMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Scouting direct DPS class, based on surface crawling. 85 1.0/0.98 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Shoot snakes that crawl on most surfaces. Redirect all of the active snakes to the current spot you are aiming at. Turns wall crawling on and off. Can crawl on walls and ceilings.
Info Tips
Your search snakes are way more useful than simply wall crawling projectiles. A smart strategy with them is to launch as much of them you can while being chased and activating your Alt-fire to redirect them towards the person following you. You can go a lot other strategies like that by focusing on spreading them and focusing them again. Surface crawling is one of the best sneaking tools on the game. Most levels have either high corners or low walls with high ceiling, meaning you can hide or sneak through pretty much any odd angle and surprise your enemies.

SPARKMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Crowd Control support class, focused on stun. 100 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Stun shot that has high windup, can be held to delay. Explodes on impact with surfaces. 8 way spread shots that cause minor stun. None None
Info Tips
Causing damage with Spark isn't really you best strategy to get kills. Abuse your stuns (both the explisive ball and the spread sparks) to cause low but constant damage, since you don't need to be too precise to hit them. You will not gain that much from holding your shots to the max. Even though it has a delay, the ability to hold the shot is best used to bait your enemy into reacting to it and hit them when they wasted their efforts to dodge your fluke.

DOC
ROBOT
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Transforming copy class focused on varying strategies. 100 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A medium speed shot. A short range scan that instantly copies the weapon of the target. None Stats change with what weapon is used.

Cannot pick up ammo but recovers it passively. Getting Unusable items will net you the Energy Balancer, that increases the speed you revocer ammo.
Info Tips
Even though Doc Robot is highly weapon reliant, the main draw of this class is the stat customization you get from the weapons. But, second to that, is that Docs have the most self suficiency, by having their ammo refill passively. Combinations of both effects are pretty much your main method of fighting. Since Docs don't have that much in regards to items, they are pretty much reliant on their stats. Knowing that, you can look for the weapons that give you the most for each situation. Cut, Quick and Spring have some pretty specificly high stats that are extremely useful for you on those cases. You can look for more though.
