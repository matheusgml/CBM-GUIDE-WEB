The Other category covers characters from games that haven't yet been fully featured on the mod or belong to games with only a few new additions.


BLADEMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Spread direct DPS class, focused on wide attacks 85 0.8/0.78 13
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Shoots blades on either a Vertical or Horizontal spread. Dash to cling into walls. While clung to a wall will sends extra projectiles. Attacking while air dashing out of a wall will do a strong melee slash. None Look down and jump to cancel wall clinging.
Info Tips
Keep in mind that the Melee Slash has overall the same damage as hitting all 3 blades of a normal attack, and as such, it is way more useful for shield/armor users, since it allows you to bypass their defenses with the ripping damage of the melee slash, while for every other situation, a simple strike from close range will suffice. You can rapidly chain walljumps, but it is not that usefull to do that offensively, as you will mostly waste more ammo faster, and will not reap as much benefit from the extra wide projectiles you get while wall clinging.

Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Throws your faucet handle as a boomerang. If your shield is up, it will fly with the faucet handle. Summons the Water Shield that can tank shots from enemies and damage them. If you already have one, expands or contracts your water shield. Explodes your water shield. None

Pump is pretty powerful on team fights thanks to their useful shield. Since they can control their size and reach, dealing with multiple targets is mostly a matter of positioning over aiming. Don't forget that the wider your shield, the less likely it is to absord shots, since the space in between water balls gets larger too. Learn to work your shield size to the situation you find yourself in.

COMMANDO
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Space control off-tank class, focused on overwhelming terrain. 155 0.6/0.58 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Commando Missile: Sends a missile that can be guided.

Commando Mine: Sends a mine that can stick to any surface.

Upon explosion, they will act in different ways depending on the surface.

- Floor: Sends a 4 way waves.

- Walls: Sends a spread of 3 balls that each sends waves.

- Ceilings: Sends a 4 way spread of balls that each sends waves.		 A body dive that causes waves of energy to explode through the ground. Activates your mines or redirect your missiles to where you are aiming at. None
Info Tips
Commando is a great demolition type class, so the most important thing to look over when you are in a match with them is that they get a lot of use for their explosions. People being baited into the traps is the main tactic to go forward, as Commando can't really work for prolongued periods of time thanks to the expensive shots. If you are not specifically hunting a single target, it is always better to use the wall and ceiling versions of your explosions. Those energy balls increase your area coverage to tremendous levels.

Description Role Health Speed Jump
Chase protector class, focused breaking engagement. 65 0.69/0.67 12
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Sends a glod of coolant that becomes a bunch of spikes if it hits a surface. The spikes cause more damage then the glob. Tap once to activate your skating ability. It will increase your movement speed and any enemy that touches your trail will slide across it towards you. Can only be activate if you still have a shield up. It is extremely useful if you need an emergency escape and is not in a good place to use the skating trail.

MERCURY

URANUS
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Ramming tank class, focused on pick offs 185 0.5/0.48 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Pick up a rock. Pressing it again throws it forward. It spreads on contact. It you instead press Alt-Fire with a rock in hand, you will instead break it, causing bits to fly around you. Charges up Rage to ram forward. Pressing Main Fire will make you slam the ground causing AOE damage and launching enemies upwards. None Damage increases Uranus' Rage.
Info Tips
Your Ram move can either cause ram damage if you just touch your opponents, or capture them and cause impact damage once your hit a wall carrying them with you. It is important to learn how to use that properly to take targets away from their team for easy kills. Your ground pound can instantly cancel your Ram, and the AOE damage is overall good. You don't need to keep your ram ammo charged to the max at all times, because there is not much use, specially because if you get it at max, it will instantly start the attack, preventing you from doing delays or faints.

MEGA
WATER S
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Anti-melee protector DPS class, focused on disrupting close combat 100 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Rapid fire arrows. Summons a pillar of water that will stun foes if they are under low ceilings and catapults enemies and yourself upward. Activates Water Shield. It lowers recieved damage. Your Alt-fire becomes a water hose that pushes enemies back. The Water Shield will build pressure with time, and pressing item again it will explode pushing enemies back. None
Info Tips
Your shield is extremely useful as the ultimate disengage tool. Beyond the added tankiness, you also gain the water hose, a strong jet of water that will fling your opponents away from you with extreme force, and having a fully build up shield pressure will instantly cause a huge push away explosion with a big radius. Low ceilings are your friend when using the shieldless Alt-Fire. The water tower can will continuously cause damage to your target and they can be chained together to keep them from moving out of it. Also important to note that it is both a great utility move, allowing you to fling yourself around, and a great disengage tool, throwing your target up in the air, giving yourself some seconds to run away.

Bounce: Shoots a bouncing shot in rapid fire.		 Compacts into your spikes to parry damage, than rolls forward to cause heavy ram damage. Press Alt-fire again to cancel raming. None None

QUINT
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Bouncing direct DPS class, focused on diving on your targets 100 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Walking: Fires a basic shot.

Sakugarne: Pogos forward causing a shotwave. Landing on an enemy will make you bounce.		 Walking: Dashes forward.

Sakugarne: Fly straight up and drop causing a huge AOE attack.		 None Jumping affects Sakugarne's reach.
Info Tips
Sakugarne's damage is extremely good and it can cause damage in multiple ways. Each time you land with it, it will cause a minor AOE quake, that causes more damage the closer the target is to the center. The Main Fire version of the landing causes a faster lunge and bigger explosion with heavier damage. Both versions can cause Bounce damage if you land on the targets head, being an extremely damaging attack that can be used to chain it with other kinds of attacks. The Alt-Fire causes a huge AOE boulder avalanche but it can also cause huge ripping damage if you land on someone with it. While out of combat, abuse the super high mobility of your Dash and Sakugarne's natural bounciness. You don't need to spend ammo to move through most obstacles, and learning how to properly move around them with both of your abilities without using the Sakugarne ammo.

RA THOR
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Build up indirect DPS class, focused on becoming stronger with kills 115 0.89/0.86 12
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Your main fire changes depending on how many energy bars you have stored:

0 Bars: A small energy projectile.

1 Bar: A faster projectile that explodes on contact.

2 Bars: 2 waving projectiles.

3 Bars: 3 spreading energy balls.

4 Bars: 4 spreading energy balls plus a giant energy ball in the middle.		 A dive that will grab your target. It can be followed up by a throw (main fire) or a driver that can (alt-fire) that can burry your target. Consume an energy bar to turn your next main fire shot into a Dark Ball, that will cause ripping damage and explode when it hits a surface. Can double jump.

Builds up energy with time.

Every time you kill someone they drop an energy ghost, that you can absorb for a full energy bar.

Upon being killed drops a part of the Sinistar Emblem. If a player can get all 6 parts of the emblem, they will get lifesteal from their attacks for as long as they stay alive.
Info Tips
Once Ra Thor starts racking up kills, they are a weapon of mass destruction. Just the spread alone of the later stages can make it very hard to trade against this class, but still, the access to more Dark Balls is where it is really becomes great. Don't get stingy with Dark Balls if you feel like you can grant a kill. The energy regained from the kill will more than make up for the use of the ball, specially if you can use it on crowded areas.

Description Role Health Speed Jump
Killstreak indirect DPS class, focused on building up an arsenal 100 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Shoots your current weapon:

Pistol: Small rapid shot;

Shotgun: Spread shot;

Gatlinggun: Rapid fire gun;

Spreadgun: A dense spread shot;

Missile Launcher: A missile that explodes on contact;
