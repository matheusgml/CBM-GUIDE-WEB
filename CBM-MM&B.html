CLASS BASED MODIFICATION GUIDE

The Mega Man & Bass category covers the characters from the original Mega Man & Bass/Rockman & Forte game.


DYNAMO
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Crowd Control support, focused on dealing with multiple allies and enemies. 85 0.85/0.83 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Scans around you to tag enemies. Tags can be stacked up to 4 times. Releasing will cause you to send a thunder storm. Pressing Alt-fire will send homing orbs of eletricity that don't need to be tagged on foes. Shoots an electrode forward that causes more damage on the electric points. Recharges health. Can be used on allies through AOE. Is fueled by screws. Can pick up screws.
Info Tips
Dynamo's play style depend a lot on if you are alone or in between friends. If you are alone, you will be playing keepaway, trying to harass with your low charge Main Fire and hidding to heal yourself. If you are with a team, you can count on them for cover while you tag everyone for maximum damage and heavling them when necessary. If you really are in a situation where close combat is out of the table, you can charge your Main Fire and release your homing orbs from a safe distance. Even though their homing is not that strong, they have enough damage to make up for the pretty low charge speed.

COLDMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Line Pusher protector class, focused on blocking. 135 0.62/0.6 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Creates a solid ice object that can be pushed with Alt-fire and they will bounce on contact. User and allies can ride on it. Walls crack over time. Melee shoulder bash. You can aim the shoulder bash to send ice walls upwards. If the wall is cracked before you push it, it will move faster. Fires a cold trap on the targeted location, freezing everyone hit in place. None
Info Tips
Cold is primarily going to throw enemies off. Your walls will ricochet on solid objects and slide on floors and can hit multiple targets before dissolving. Knowing that, you can create room traps easily by leaving a few walls laying around and throwing new walls in between them when enemies try to navigate it. Your wall can be used to send allies through the map by letting them ride on it when you push them around. Including upwards. Remember that.

GROUND
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Flanking off-tank class, focused on terrain dominance. 115 0.64/0.62 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Fires a giant drill that falls after a short distance. Will split upon hitting a surface. Turns into Drill Tank. Pressing it again will cancel it. While in this form you can press Main Fire to dig into the ground, allowing you to move inside surfaces. Pressing Main Fire again will allow you to dig into the ceiling. Pressing Main Fire after that allows you to summon Giga Drills under you. Force your drills to split. They will lock on a horizontal trajectory. None
Info Tips
Ground's main gimmick is the wide array of approach methods. The Main Fire has enough power to cause trouble in close range, but you can still use it's spread to disperse enemies from long range. But still the best choice for approach in crowded areas is the use of tank form, in either the ground or ceiling. Just be warned that you will cause a lot of noise, so don't hope for a stealthy approach. Use the horizontal recentering of your drill's spread to surprise enemies with strange angles. Also, holding your spread to the last second can give you a better chance of surprising your targets.

PIRATE
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Trapper off-tank class, focused on mine placement. 125 0.65/0.63 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Fires guided mines that fly torwards your eyesight. Hold to keep controling them and release to make them stop. Can keep 3 of them on the field before exploding them with the next press. Sends a jet stream of water that pushes foes and mines away. While encased in your bubble, you can use it during a jump to ram into enemies. Changes to a rapidfire bubble gun while underwater. Toggles Bubble On/Off.

Bubble reduces damage taken.		 Can steal items from the ground.
Info Tips
Pirate's main method of combat is minelaying. Use the remote control of the mines to either place them on surprise spots or place them close to you and use you water cannon to push them into your enemies. Save your shield to cases where you are being focused. Premature explosions (Activated by trying to lay a mine after having 3 on the field) can be of great utility, since they have a good staying power and radius of effect. Use this tight passages to prevent enemies from passing.

BURNER
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Hunting direct DPS class, focused on rapid fire damage.. 65 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A flamethrower. Will send singular flames when out of ammo. Changes to a stream of turbulence while underwater. Dashes through the ground while keeping your ammo refilling at the normal pace. If used in the air will dive releasing a huge spread of fire. Plants stun mines on the ground. None
Info Tips
With Burner you will be dashing around the map, harassing people with you flamethrower and only getting in direct combat when you have a chance of killing them in a single magazine. Your dive is a good choice for both escapes and dispersions, but you will be mostly counting on your traps to get your enemies locked for you to harass them. While underwater, you get a more direct stream from your Main Fire instead of a wide spread. This is not only important to rethink your fighting style, you can use this to surprise people in chases.

MAGICMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Tricky indirect DPS class, focused on confusing your enemy. 90 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Cards: Throws a card that steals life.

Ball: Throws a ball that teleports you forward sends a flock of homing birds that causes damage.		 Cards: Rapid fire of cards that cause light stun.

Ball: Throws a ball that fakes a forward teleport and sends a flock of homing birds that causes damage.		 None None
Info Tips
Magic fights by mixing up. Both the life stealer and stun magic cards serve their own purposes, and holding an enemy in place while you recharge your life stealer is one of the basic plays for this combo. Save your teleport balls for cases where you need high mobility, since they use a lot of ammo. Fake teleport balls can be used for scouting too. Always remember that the pidgeon barrage from your teleport balls will come from the opposite place you end up. So if you throw the true teleport ball, you will teleport forward and the pidgeons will come off where you where before, and the fake teleport ball will end with you where you where before but the pidgeons will fly out of the place you hit with the ball.

KING
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Variable protector class, focused on both offensive and defensive gameplay.. 115 0.73/0.71 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Axe: Sideway slash. If you use it while you hold jump you will do a faster vertical slash that reaches further.

Shield: Raises shield to block shots. Can be held to block for longer. Shield breaks after some use and needs to cool down.		 Axe: Rams forward spinning your axe and causing damage.

Shield: Winds up a X shaped shot that stuns enemies.		 Nope None
Info Tips
King depends completely on what your team needs most at the moment (or what the situations ask for). Axe mode has a lot of offensive utilities with great mid to short range tools of combat alongside a great chase tool. The Shield mode on the other hand is amazing for keeping yourself and your teamates alive, alongside with a pretty good damage output. The Shield mode has a limit for how much it can be used and it will have to cool down. But it doesn't mean that you will have to just drop it without a using it the most. When the Shield exausts while you hold it, if will explode and fly forward, causing 25 damage, making it a pretty good way of closing in on people.
