CLASS BASED MODIFICATION GUIDE - ROLES

Each class is scored on 3 main stats from a pool of a total of 20 points, spread around to reflect how good they are at each specific stat.


Stat Description
Damage is the potential for damage. They generally have either highly damaging attacks or can use attacks in quick sucession without a lot of drawbacks.
Sustain is the ability to stay alive. They generally have either high Health stats or abilities that either deny or absord damage.
Utility is the diversity in skills. They are everything that a class can do other than cause damage. Mobility, buffs, debuffs, shields, etc.

Also, according to each class' score, they are also assigned a role, that is, the closest to what their stats, but they are not set in stone, so with enough know-how you can play outside of the role assigned here.


Role Description
Copy is a special category for classes that have the Copy Ability. This means that they start with a less stabilished kit, but can add new tools to their arsenal through finding them in the map or in killing enemies in LMS games.
Direct DPS is the category for classes that can keep a constant stream of damage without too much oversight. They generally are always ready for combat and can outdamage most of their enemies through smart use of their tools.
Indirect DPS is the category for classes that will mostly excel if they can properly build up their advantage. They depend mostly on finding or creating situations where they will have the upper hand and cause a lot of damage with that.
Support is the category for classes that can benefit allies in amazing ways. They aren't that powerful if alone, but can literally change the tide of most battles if they can use their skills on team games.
Off-Tank is the category for classes that can take on direct combat constantly. A combination of sustain and damage makes those classes most likely to survive a direct combat, and they are great at harassing enemies.
Protector is the category for classes that have special defensive moves to benefits from enemies wasting attacks on them. They generally have either high Health stats and/or can render enemy attacks useless with their specialized kits.
Tank is the category for classes that are extremely good at staying alive. They have the highest Health stats on the game, and are competent enought at fighting, but they will mostly be kind the of character that will either take full atention to kill or team work to deal with them rapidly.
