CENTAUR
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
AOE direct DPS class, focused on hit and run tactics. 85 1.0/0.6 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A small shot that splits on surfaces. An AOE attack that stun closer enemies. Short range teleport. None
Info Tips
Centaur is a master of AOE damage. Circle strafing and shooting at surfaces will grand that you will keep hitting targets with odd angles using the split of your Main Fire. Save your Alt-fire for those cases where you are surrounded and your teleportation to when you need a granted escape route or a way to trespass gaps. Your teleport ignores gravity. Learn the total distance you can get with it and use it to skip parts of the maps for your advantage, cornering enemies from unexpected angles.

FLAMEMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Terrain Control indirect DPS class, focused on denying space. 100 0.8/0.78 12
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A small projectile that leaves fire pillars where it hits. Flame spread that bounces on walls. None None
Info Tips
With Flame you should try your best start positioning fire pillars before you start a fight. Since they last long and can put on wall, you can force your target into bad situations just by forcing them to navigate your fire. If you can, start with your Alt-Fire. Your Alt-Fire may not look like it, but it can bounce back on surfaces, meaning that you make denser firewalls on corridors and closed spaces.

KNIGHT
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Aggro Pulling protector class, focused on denying attacks. 115 0.65/0.57 12
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A morning star that ricochets on surfaces. Raise your shield and bash your enemies. Can deflect some attacks. Is invulnerable during bash. None Can shield projectiles in front of you.
Info Tips
Knight is pretty good at making enemies waste their attacks. Start a trade trying to make them use their strongest attacks and shield bash them parrying it all. Remember that the push from the bash can also deny melee classes. While you are not attacking, you will recieve a passive from your shield, and it is important to memorize that, as you can save your shield bash for more useful circunstances.

PLANTMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Sustain Protector class, focused on stealing health. 85 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A spreading rapid fire gun. Can heal damage if you are in T/LMS. Raises Plant Barrier. It causes contact damage and heals you proportional to the amount dealt. None Passively regenerates health (Except T/LMS).
Info Tips
Plant doesn't have that much health alone, but this class more than makes up for it by being able to heal constantly and more rapidly in battle. Hug some mid damage enemies or tank a higher blow to save an ally and get out before you are killed, allowing you to survive a lot of dire situations. Since this class works differently on LMS, it is important to remember those differences and adapt your playstyle accordingly.

TOMAHAWK
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Tricky direct DPS class, focused on countering angled movement. 100 0.89/0.87 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Fires double tomahawks that fly on vertical arcs. Their damage increase after some distance. Can crawl on the floor/ceiling. Sends a spread of feathers that arcs horizontaly. None None
Info Tips
With Tomahawk, you don't need to focus on hitting your foes with both projectiles of your Main Fire. The added damage is negligible from it and it is easier to just use your distance and angles to hit your enemies safely. Use your feathers for those classes with high horizontal mobility in the same way. Your Silver Tomahawks have an interesting property where they will fly completely vertically if you aim almost vertically, and this can actually help you hit some targets that are trying to focus on you in those angles.

WINDMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Pulling protector class, focused on countering keep away. 125 0.7/0.68 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Sends 3 waving projectiles. Damage stacked from the 3 shots is lower than total damage. Sends 2 Wind Storms forward that crawl on the ground and throw the target closer to you. Toggles Flight. Flight can be cancelled with Alt-Fire to dive on enemies. None
Info Tips
Wind's projectiles benefit a lot from hitting with the 3 of them, so keeping close is key. Alternate in between your Alt-Fire to bring your enemies close and spamming of the Main Fire. Your flight is better saved for cases where you have to chase people. If you ever need an easy way of pushing your foes away from you, rapidly activate flight and cancel it with your Alt-Fire. It will send an AOE attack that will push foes aways.

YAMATO
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Duelist off-tank class, focused on rapid attacks. 125 0.666/0.666 13
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Sends a pread of spear heads that will stay on the ground if they don't hit a target. Picking them refills their ammo. Rapid melee damage. User is slowed while using this attack. Can pull back the spear heads that are on the floor. None None
Info Tips
Yamato's main draw is the speed of this class' attack. You get close using your Main Fire and start holding your Alt-fire once you are close enough. Remember to shoot on angles that are directed close to the place where you want to go so that you keep recharging your Main Fire by using the dropped spearheads. Even though it is a straight forward class, you can still surprise your enemies with help from your teammates. Try to flank people to compensate for your movement speed and get some pretty fast kills.
