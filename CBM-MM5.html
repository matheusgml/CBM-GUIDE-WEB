The Mega Man 5 category covers the characters from the original Mega Man 5/Rockman 5 game and it's remakes/spin-offs.


GRAVITY
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Mobility Denying indirect DPS class, focused on gravity tricks. 100 0.8/0.78 15
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Normal speed shot. AOE attack that slams enemies down or up depending on your orientation. Changes your gravity. None
Info Tips
Gravity is a class that jukes around by playing with the gravity itself. Learning to play around with mixups of throwing up and down allows you to keep your opponents disoriented and unfocused. Throw your enemies off using the upwards gravity pull to grant some saves for your teammates and deny flying classes of their mobility using your throw down.

WAVEMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Flanking indirect DPS class, focused on moving around your targets. 100 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Spawns a wave of water on the target location that moves foward. Will spawn a wave that moves backwards if you point it at a wall. Normal speed harpoon shots. Uses a Hook Shot that can keep you clinging to surfaces. Can be canceled by pressing Item again. You can hook yourself to enemies and allies alike. None
Info Tips
Wave is a class with surprising high mobility (but limited use of it) making it a great class for those duelists that like to leave their targets lost. Combine precise summoning of your waves with your Hook-Shot and harpoons for an easy victory. Your Water Wave can do multiple tics of damage if you are able to pinpoint at the targets specific location, and it is pretty reliable as an area denial tool.

STONEMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Close Range off-tank class, focused on pressure. 155 0.8/0.78 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Launches spiralling projectiles around you.

Can be used to cancel the aerial animation of the Alt-Fire.		 Chargeable leap that sends a homing crawling projectile.

More charge means more height on the leap.

Can be used to stomp on enemies.		 None If you are to recieve Letal Damage, you will instead become a Stone Core, and if you survive until your ammo bar if full, you will return to life with part of your health back.
Info Tips
Stone is a class that can cause astounding pressure on enemies by hugging them with their huge amount of life and throwing Power Stones around. Use the leap as a starter and the confusion caused by that to get close and cause a lot of damage. Just tapping Alt-fire at close range will allow you to leap on the targets head easily, allow you to do extra damage on enemies that are too mobile for the standard strategy.

GYROMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Flying direct DPS class, focused on angled shots. 85 0.87/0.85 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Gyro Attack can slide on the ground and the ceiling. Toggles flight. None None
Info Tips
Since your flight isn't that fast, you will be using it for preparing your positioning to get better angles to shoot your targets. Don't be afraid to use your flight move around though, getting used to getting in and out of flight is the best way to get the most of this skill. The fact that your Gyro Attack can crawl on the ground is a good way to help orient yourself in ways that facilitate hitting your target when using your flight to juke them.

STARMAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Duelist protector class, focused on close range trading. 115 0.75/0.72 12
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Raises Star Chrasher (A shield that causes contact damage and decrease the damage you recieve to 75%). Press Fire or Alt-Fire to shoot the shield forward. Creates a wormhole that releases a shower of stars. None None
Info Tips
Singling people out with Star is important as a way to outtrade most of them thanks to the armor you gain from your Star Crash. The constant AOE damage it causes is perfect for hitting squichier enemies that are focused on rapid damage dealing. The Star Barrage from your alt-fire comes with a delay. Even though it is not that damaging, it can throw people out of your back while you wait for your shield to recharge. Corridors are easy picks for this strategy.

CHARGE
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Duelist off-tank class, focused on out-evading opponents. 125 0.8/0.4 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Ram into foes. Precision increases damage. Pumps coal, causing AOE damage around you and lobs a small piece of coal forward as a projectile. Denies damage recieved while flashing red. None None
Info Tips
Charge's main attack is pretty damaging and but shouldn't be wasted all in a single combo. If you don't kill your enemy before your energy runs out, you will be out of ways to defend yourself, as you will be unable to dash, parry or attack. Knowing the best mix of evading, parrying and attacking will allow you to conserve energy. Most times, using your charge to evade is better than parrying because you will be using less energy and will be in a different position from before, throwing your oponent off. Save parries for those times where dodging is not really possible.

NAPALM
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
High Damage direct DPS class, focused on all in attacks. 100 0.89/0.5 10
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Launches a barrages of bouncing bombs forward. Launches a barrage of missiles. None None
Info Tips
Napalm's main strategy is cornering your target and emptying your magazine on them before they can react. Abuse your high speed to dodge them while you recharge if they somehow survive. Just remember to not go after multiple targets or you will be defenseless. The bounciness of your Main Fire is extremely useful for tight spaces, as it will allow a single bomb multiple chances to hit your targets. Strafe shots are also your best friend in those cases.

CRYSTAL
MAN
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Sniper off-tank class, focused on room clearing. 125 0.7/0.68 13
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
Launches a huge projectile that ricochets on surfaces. Can explode the projectile into smaller ricocheting bits by pressing Main Fire again. The more you charge, the more bits will spawn. The trajectory of the bits follows the current position of your aim, so you can control them to fly in odd angles. A small ricocheting projetile. None None
Info Tips
While Crystal's kit excels in tight spaces, you can pretty much break access to entire rooms if you know how to preperly place your Crystal Eyes. Releasing them prematuriley should only be used on escapes, as your unreleased Crystal Eye does enough damage in close range. If you are close range and in open spaces, don't waste your time on trying to get the best Crystal Eyes shots. You Crystal Bits are way faster and preciser for those situations, throwing Crystal Eyes only when you are sure you can hit.

DARK
MAN 1
Description Role Health Speed Jump
Duelist off-tank class, focused on rapid-fire damage. 155 0.65/0.4 0
Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra
A barrage of normal shots. A barrage of small shots that arc downward and give a small explosion. None The less life you have, the faster you shoot.

Pressing jump will make you boost up and hover.

Can autoclimb small ledges.
Info Tips
Dark1 has a sweet spot of life/damage ratio that is around 1/3 of your life. Only trade close if you are at full health, and you will probably outdamage them, but if you can stay at mid range, this 1/3 health will allow you maximum damage output with safety. Even though it may not look like it, Dark1 has actually extremely good mobility. The hovering instead of jump allows you to more precisely cross certain spaces without too much trouble compared to other classes.

MAN 3

Main Fire Alt-Fire Item Extra

Learn to snipe people with this class, because the damage gain from the distance is absolutely worth the trouble.
